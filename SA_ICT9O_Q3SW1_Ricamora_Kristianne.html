Barbie: Life in the Dreamhouse



Synopsis of the Movie

Barbie: Life in the Dreamhouse is like a reality show. The characters have confessionals in each episode. The show is set at a fictional version of Malibu.





Barbie

She is the main character of the show. She has three siblings in this show, Skipper, Stacey and Chelsea. She is a fashion icon with more than a hundred jobs.

Ken

He is Barbie's boyfriend. In some episodes, he competes with Ryan for Barbie's attention. He is shown in the show as the supportive boyfriend of Barbie.

Raquelle

She is Barbie's frenemy. She tries to outshine Barbie in a lot of episodes. Her brother is Ryan, who has a crush on Barbie.

Ryan

He is Raquelle's brother. He likes to make music. He has a crush on Barbie and tries to impress her.

Nikki

She is the sassy best friend of Barbie. She doesn't hold back on her opinions. She is close friends with Teresa and Barbie.

Teresa

She is the ditzy best friend of Barbie. She is an airhead. She is close friends with Nikki and Barbie.

Midge

She is the childhood friend of Barbie. She acts old, because she is an old doll. When she met up again with Barbie in the show, she recieved a makeover, and Ryan started crushing on her.
